Let's Talk Intentional Walks

I had to make a separate page just for intentional walks because Barry Lamar Bonds is a man who can be referred to as the Greek God of intentional Walks. I want to start this section of my shrine to Barry with a brief little story. One time, the Giants were playing a game against the Arizona Diamondbacks. Barry came to the plate in the ninth inning with the bases loaded and the Giants were down by two. Watch what happened next.

That's right. Arizona opted to give the Giants a free run during the 9th inning rather than taking the risk of pitching to Barry Lamar Bonds. Unreal.

That is not all though, there are tons of intentional walk numbers from the career of Barry Lamar Bonds that will blow you away. Here's a few:

  • To this day, Barry Lamar Bonds still has more career intentional walks than the entire Tampa Bay Rays franchise in the history of their existence
  • Barry Lamar Bonds is the only player in mlb to record over 30 intentional walks in 3 or more seasons. He has recorded over 30 intentional walks in ten different seasons
  • In the years 2001-2004, the league leaders in intentional walks went as followed:
    1. Barry Lamar Bonds (284)
    2. The St. Louis Cardinals (260)
    3. The Montreal Expos (252) (rest in peace)
    4. The Philadelphia Phillies (237)
    5. The Arizona Diamondbacks (232) Barry had more intentional walks than any other TEAM in that stretch.
  • In his career, Barry Lamar Bonds walked unintentionally 1870 times and intentionally 688 times. That means that Barry walked 43.6 miles in his career

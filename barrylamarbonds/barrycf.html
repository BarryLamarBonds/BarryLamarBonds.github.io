The Greatest of All Time

Go ahead and take a look here at some of Barry Lamar Bonds' career feats and some other obscure facts that help make the case as to why he is, in fact, the GOAT.

  • Barry Lamar Bonds is the only member of the 500 home run/500 stolen base club
  • Barry Lamar Bonds is also the only member of the 400 home run/400 stolen base club
  • Barry Lamar Bonds finished his career with 8 gold gloves, which is the most all time by any corner outfielder
  • Turn every single one of Barry Lamar Bonds' career home runs into a strike out and his career OBP will still sit at .384, which is higher than that of Alex Rodriguez
  • Barry Lamar Bonds batted .571 in his career on bunt attempts (the man does it all)
  • Barry Lamar Bonds has more career MVP awards than any two Hall of Famers combined
  • In 2004, Barry Lamar Bonds reached base 376 times. He had 373 at-bats that season
  • From 2001-2004, Barry Lamar Bonds played in 573 games and safely reached base in 539 of those games. That is 94 percent for those wondering
